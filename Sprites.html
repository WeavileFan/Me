Sprites

Below are some sprites I have made. I only have 2 right now but I make them sometimes.

How do I make them?

I use a program called Piskel. The two sprites I have so far are Gen 1 recolors. I uploaded the gen 1 sprites into the program and then in another page, I get all the HEX codes of the colors and swap all the old Gen 1 colors for the Gen 3 sprite colors.

If you would like to use these sprites for your own webpages/content, feel free! (With credit to me/links to this site, of course) Do not use these sprites for commercial use unless you get written permission from me.
